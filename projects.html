Throwback Hack is service that allows you a be reminded about a thing you experienced from the past! It really does feel great and our goal with this website is to provide you, the user with the opportunity to relive experiences from the past in accordance to your likings.

Tuber

Tuber is an Uber service for tutoring. Tuber allows the user to find many different tutors in your area for the ease of students. The user can search by price, subject, and distance away from them. Tuber removes and skips the annoying process of setting up a meeting time and location with the tutor. As a result, both students and tutors will have saved countless hours.

Re-Hack-Ilation

Re.hack.litation is a software for the Leap Motion that helps patients rediscover their lost motor skills.
With Re.hack.litaiion, patients can complete different games, which allows them to move around different objects using their muscle movement.
