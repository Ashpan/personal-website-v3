94
Have you ever been reminded about a thing you experienced from the past! It really does feel great and at throwback hack our goal is to provide you, the user with the opportunity to relive experiences from the past in accordance to your liking. Check it out by clicking on the image! Tuber is an Uber service for tutoring. Tuber allows the user to find many different tutors in your area for the ease of students. The user can search by price, subject, and distance away from them. Tuber removes and skips the annoying process of setting up a meeting time and location with the tutor. As a result, both students and tutors will have saved countless hours. Tuber was created by four highschool students at the HackWestern 2 event. Tuber is an android native application, it was created in Android Studio and uses Parse's API to push and fetch data as well as the geographical tagging feature. 80
